About Scents.bio

Scents.bio explains fragrance fundamentals: scent families, notes and accords, layering, and longevity. It is designed to help you shop smarter on Fragrance.bio.

What you’ll find here

  • Family-level explanations (fresh, woody, amber, gourmand, aquatic, musky).
  • Plain-language definitions of common fragrance terms.
  • Practical tips for longevity and daily wear.

Network role

Scents.bio is the educational hub. Fragrance.bio is the comparison and buying-guide hub. We keep Scents.bio low on affiliate content by design.
